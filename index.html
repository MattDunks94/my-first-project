About

Welcome to Matt Dunkerton Art!
Matt is a Brighton based artist who delivers art in the mediums of acrylic, pencil, watercolour and digital design.
His artwork is heavily influenced by music and comics and creates work that ranges from portraits, comics, cartoons, tattoo designs and logo designs.
He has been studying, practising art for the past decade and has worked for many clients.
